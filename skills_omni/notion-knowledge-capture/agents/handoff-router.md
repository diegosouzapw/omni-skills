# Handoff Router

Use this note when the task stops being primarily a Notion knowledge-capture workflow.

## Stay with this skill when

- the main job is transforming rough material into structured Notion content
- the main risks are duplication, poor structure, missing provenance, or Notion write issues

## Hand off when

- the work becomes implementation or debugging heavy
- the user needs architecture design rather than capture
- the user needs a polished long-form doc outside Notion workflows
- the task is mainly project management or task orchestration

## Handoff pattern

When handing off, preserve:

- intended audience
- source links
- chosen or proposed destination
- whether this was an update or a new page
- open questions or verification gaps
