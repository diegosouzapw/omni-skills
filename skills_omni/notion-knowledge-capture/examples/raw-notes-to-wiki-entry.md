# Example: Raw Notes to Wiki Entry

## Source material

Scattered notes describe an internal service, its purpose, common terms, and where it fits in the platform.

## Recommended output shape

- Summary: What the service is and why it exists.
- Overview: Main responsibilities and boundaries.
- Key terms: Internal vocabulary and abbreviations.
- How it fits: Upstream and downstream systems.
- Related pages: Architecture, runbooks, and ownership pages.
- Sources: Notes, diagrams, and canonical references.

## Operator note

Do not paste all raw notes if they are messy or repetitive. Synthesize them into a cleaner conceptual page and keep links to the originals.
