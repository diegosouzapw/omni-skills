# Visual QA Worksheet

Use this to review a page quickly across breakpoints.

## First viewport

- Is the brand or product unmistakable?
- Is there one dominant visual anchor?
- Is the CTA obvious?
- Does the hero feel full-bleed, boxed, or accidental?

## Hierarchy

- Can the page be understood by scanning headlines only?
- Does each section have one job?
- Is any section repeating the same message?

## Restraint

- Are cards necessary?
- Are there too many accents, borders, or shadows?
- Is any decorative chrome compensating for weak composition?

## Accessibility

- Is text over imagery readable?
- Are focus states visible?
- Does motion remain tolerable and optional where needed?

## Responsive review

- What breaks first on mobile?
- Does the image crop still support the text?
- Does the page still feel intentional at tablet width?
