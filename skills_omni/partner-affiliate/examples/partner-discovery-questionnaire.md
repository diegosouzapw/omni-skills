# Example: Partner Discovery Questionnaire

Use these prompts to collect the minimum information required for a design recommendation.

1. What does the product do, and who is the ideal customer?
2. What is the price point or average contract value?
3. Is the business self-serve, sales-led, or hybrid?
4. What outcome should the partner program drive in the next two quarters?
5. Do any customers, agencies, consultants, creators, or platforms already send demand today?
6. Which adjacent tools or ecosystems are most common in the customer workflow?
7. What internal resources exist for partner management, finance review, compliance review, and technical support?
8. What tracking systems are already in place?
9. What partner disputes or channel conflicts already exist?
10. What would make this program unsuccessful even if signups increased?
