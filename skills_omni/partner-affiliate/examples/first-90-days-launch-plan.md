# Example: First 90 Days Launch Plan

## Days 1-15
- Confirm program objective and primary motion
- Finalize payable event definitions
- Draft attribution and conflict rules
- Complete legal and finance dependency list
- Prepare core onboarding assets

## Days 16-30
- Recruit a narrow pilot cohort
- Walk each partner through the first campaign or referral motion
- Test tracking and reconciliation manually
- Log all edge cases and exceptions

## Days 31-60
- Review first qualified referrals or opportunities
- Segment activation by partner type
- Tighten onboarding, messaging, and compliance gaps
- Remove or pause low-fit partners

## Days 61-90
- Expand only if attribution and payout logic are stable
- Introduce tiering or differentiated enablement if justified
- Review concentration risk and partner quality
- Decide whether to scale, redesign, or keep the motion niche
