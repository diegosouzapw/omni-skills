# Hybrid AI + Human UGC Test Plan

## Scenario

- Product: consumer wellness product
- Goal: find winning hooks fast, then scale with real creators
- Channels: TikTok and Instagram Reels
- Budget: moderate
- Constraint: avoid unsupported health claims

## Phase 1: AI concept exploration

Goal:

- generate multiple hook and script variants cheaply

Plan:

1. Draft 8-12 hook variations around one verified product angle.
2. Render a limited set of AI variants using clearly licensed assets.
3. Human-review every render for authenticity issues, claims risk, and visual defects.
4. Post or test only the strongest few variants.

Do not:

- present fabricated customer experiences as real testimonials
- use deceptive likenesses or cloned voices without permission

## Phase 2: Human creator validation

Goal:

- convert winning script structures into more trustworthy creator content

Plan:

1. Recruit 3-5 creators who match the target audience.
2. Use the creator brief template with rights and disclosure requirements.
3. Keep the top AI-discovered hook structures, but adapt them to natural creator delivery.
4. Approve concepts before full filming.

## Phase 3: Paid amplification planning

TikTok:

- if using creator-post amplification, confirm Spark Ads readiness before launch

Meta:

- if using creator identity in ads, confirm partnership-ad permissions before launch

## Phase 4: Decision review

Ask:

- Did human creators outperform AI on trust and conversion?
- Did AI still help reduce concepting time?
- Which hooks generalized across both creators and platforms?
- What rights or permissions bottlenecks slowed scale?

## Likely outcome

Use AI for ideation and pretesting, then scale with human creator content where trust, realism, and platform identity matter.
