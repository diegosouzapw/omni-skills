# Compliance Escalation Router

Use this router when the request is no longer safely handled inside the AI UGC Ads workflow.

## Escalate immediately if any of the following are true

- the user asks for legal approval or contract language
- the campaign depends on regulated claims without substantiation
- the request involves synthetic impersonation or deceptive AI media
- the platform account is restricted, suspended, or blocked
- the user wants advice on hiding sponsorship or fabricating testimonials

## Route by issue type

### Legal or regulatory uncertainty

Route to:

- legal or compliance owner
- regulated-category reviewer

### Platform account or asset restrictions

Route to:

- account admin
- platform support workflow
- business asset operations owner

### Analytics or implementation work

Route to:

- analytics implementation owner
- paid media operator
- technical integration workflow

## Safe handoff summary format

When escalating, summarize:

- objective
- platform
- content source: human / AI / hybrid
- rights status
- disclosure or claims concern
- exact blocker
- what has already been verified
