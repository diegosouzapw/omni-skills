# Meta Partnership Ads Runbook

Use this runbook for planning or troubleshooting creator amplification through Meta partnership ads and related branded content permissions.

## What this path is for

Meta partnership ads let advertisers run ads using creator identity and approved relationships, subject to current Meta requirements.

## Preflight prerequisites

Confirm:

- the creator and brand accounts are the intended identities
- the creator is willing and technically able to authorize the business relationship
- any branded content partner approval required by Meta is complete
- the intended post and placement are eligible
- sponsorship disclosure expectations are clear

## Setup sequence

1. Identify the exact creator account and post intended for amplification.
2. Confirm branded content or partnership permissions are in place using Meta's current workflow.
3. Verify the brand appears correctly as an approved business partner where required.
4. In Ads Manager, create the campaign using Meta's current partnership ad flow.
5. Confirm the correct creator identity and post are selectable before launch.

## Common failure modes

### Creator post is not selectable

Check:

- whether the creator completed the required approval flow
- whether the correct business or ad account is being used
- whether the post status or placement makes it ineligible

Fix:

- complete partner approval first
- re-check account alignment
- use a fresh eligible post if needed

### Brand and creator identities do not match the expected setup

Check:

- whether the correct Instagram or Facebook asset is connected
- whether the business partner was approved in the correct account
- whether the ad account operator has the right permissions

Fix:

- align accounts and permissions before relaunching setup

### Ad is rejected despite creator approval

Check:

- policy-sensitive claim language
- branded content disclosure gaps
- restricted category issues
- misleading testimonial or synthetic media concerns

Fix:

- resolve policy issues first
- do not continue optimization until approval blockers are addressed

## Operator notes

- Meta labels and navigation can change; validate current UI against official help-center guidance.
- Confirm permissions before asking creators to produce more assets.
- Keep a written record of which creator approved which business partner and for what campaign purpose.
