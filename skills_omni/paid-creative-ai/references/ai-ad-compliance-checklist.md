# AI Ad Compliance Checklist

Use this checklist before launch or resubmission. This is operational guidance, not legal advice.

## Core Review

- [ ] The ad does not make unsupported or exaggerated claims.
- [ ] The landing page matches the ad's offer, proof, and CTA.
- [ ] Product visuals do not materially misrepresent the real product or outcome.
- [ ] AI-generated people, scenes, or demonstrations are not misleading in context.
- [ ] The asset does not rely on prohibited before/after or similarly risky framing for sensitive categories.
- [ ] The ad does not appear deceptively manipulated.
- [ ] Required human review has happened before launch.

## AI-Specific Quality and Risk Checks

- [ ] Hands, faces, text, and motion look natural enough for public use.
- [ ] Dubbing, voice, and lip sync do not create misleading impressions.
- [ ] Any synthetic enhancement does not imply a result the product cannot deliver.
- [ ] The asset would still be defensible if a reviewer compared it directly with the landing page and product reality.

## Platform Review Prompts

### Meta

Check:

- ad standards category risk
- misleading claims
- image/video quality
- text density and legibility
- landing-page consistency

### Google

Check:

- misrepresentation risk
- manipulated media risk
- landing-page mismatch
- generated asset review
- regulated-category concerns

### TikTok

Check:

- policy category restrictions
- misleading visual edits
- prohibited or restricted claims
- native ad review requirements

## Escalate

Escalate to human legal/compliance review when:

- disclosure obligations are jurisdiction-specific
- the product is regulated
- the claim relies on legal interpretation
- the ad was repeatedly rejected for reasons that are not clearly creative-only
