## Interpretation rules

Do not treat one signal as conclusive.

### Healthy pattern
- strong disappointment score
- stable or improving retention
- expansion in the target segment
- acceptable win rate and sales cycle

### Warning pattern
- survey score looks strong but retention weakens
- pipeline grows while win rate declines
- usage stays flat but satisfaction falls
- one segment performs well while the broader market underperforms

## Sean Ellis interpretation bands

| Score | Interpretation |
| --- | --- |
| <20% | no meaningful PMF signal |
| 20-30% | weak signal |
| 30-40% | approaching PMF |
| 40-50% | practical PMF |
| 50%+ | strong PMF |

## Review outputs

At the end of each review, publish:
- what changed in the market
- what changed in the best segment
- whether the ICP narrowed, expanded, or shifted
- whether messaging needs rewriting
- whether proof claims need refreshing
- next review date and owner
