# Messaging Test Script

Use this script to test comprehension, resonance, credibility, and differentiation.

## Step 1: Show one message at a time

Present:
- one narrative paragraph, or
- one value proposition, or
- one comparison statement

Do not stack multiple messages in the same prompt.

## Step 2: Ask neutral validation questions

- In your own words, what is this product claiming to do?
- Who do you think this is for?
- What feels most relevant here?
- What feels vague or hard to believe?
- What would you want to see before trusting this claim?
- How is this different from your current alternative?
- Would you forward this to someone involved in the purchase? Why or why not?

## Step 3: Score the response

| Dimension | 1 | 3 | 5 |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Comprehension | confusing | partly clear | immediately clear |
| Relevance | low priority | somewhat relevant | urgent |
| Credibility | not believable | plausible | well supported |
| Differentiation | generic | some contrast | clearly distinct |
| Forwarding potential | would not share | maybe | would share now |

## Step 4: Interpret

- low comprehension -> simplify wording or category anchor
- low relevance -> likely wrong segment or weak pain framing
- low credibility -> improve proof, not adjectives
- low differentiation -> sharpen wedge against alternatives
- low forwarding potential -> wrong buyer altitude or low business case clarity
