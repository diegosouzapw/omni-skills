# Content-to-Conversation Playbook

This example assumes a founder posts 3 times per week and wants more qualified conversations.

## Weekly plan

### Monday: problem insight post

- Topic: a costly mistake the ICP keeps making
- CTA: ask readers whether they see the same issue
- Follow-up: reply to substantive comments and tag them as potential conversations

### Wednesday: proof or case pattern post

- Topic: a before-and-after transformation or repeatable lesson
- CTA: invite readers to DM for the checklist, framework, or example
- Follow-up: send the asset only to people who explicitly ask

### Friday: point-of-view post

- Topic: a contrarian but supportable perspective tied to the buyer problem
- CTA: ask a single question that surfaces readiness or disagreement
- Follow-up: identify serious responders for one-to-one outreach

## Daily operating rhythm

- review comments and inbound reactions
- engage with 3-5 ICP accounts genuinely
- follow up with people who opted in through comments or DMs
- log qualified conversations in CRM

## Conversion rules

Move from content to DM when the person:

- asks for more detail
- comments with a specific pain point
- references timing, budget, or an internal initiative
- engages repeatedly across multiple posts

## Review at the end of the week

- which post generated the most qualified conversations?
- which CTA produced the best follow-through?
- which DMs became meetings?
- which topics attracted the wrong audience?
