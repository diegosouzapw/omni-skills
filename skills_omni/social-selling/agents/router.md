# Social Selling Router

Use this note to hand off when the task moves outside the core social-selling workflow.

## Route to adjacent skills when:

- the user needs email-first outbound or multichannel sequence design beyond social DMs
- the task becomes CRM implementation, automation engineering, or API integration
- the request is primarily ad operations or paid distribution
- the work is mainly copywriting for large content programs rather than social-selling workflow design
- the request asks for policy interpretation, legal advice, or risky tooling approval

## Stay in this skill when:

- the task is about LinkedIn profile optimization for selling
- the user needs a safer DM or connection-request workflow
- the user needs Sales Navigator research and prioritization guidance
- the user needs content-to-conversation planning and attribution
- the user needs founder or employee advocacy operating guidance

## Safety note

Do not route into tooling recommendations that imply scraping, bulk automation, or platform workarounds without explicit human review.
