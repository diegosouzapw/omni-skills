# Example: Sales-Led with Value-Before-Purchase

## Scenario

An enterprise analytics platform sells into regulated organizations with long buying cycles and multiple stakeholders.

## Recommendation

- Primary motion: sales-led
- Proof mechanism: guided sandbox plus ROI calculator
- Support path: security review packet, tailored demo, stakeholder workshop
- KPI focus: opportunity progression, sandbox engagement quality, cycle time by segment

## Why

- buyer complexity is high
- independent self-serve evaluation is not enough
- proof must include technical and business validation
