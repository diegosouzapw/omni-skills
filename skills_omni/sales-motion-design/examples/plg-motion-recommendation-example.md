# Example: PLG Motion Recommendation

## Scenario

A workflow SaaS targets small teams at low ACV. Users can sign up alone and reach value quickly with templates.

## Recommendation

- Primary motion: pure PLG
- Entry model: opt-in free trial or freemium, depending marginal cost
- Focus: reduce TTFV with prebuilt templates and sample data
- KPIs: activation rate, time to activation, free-to-paid conversion, team invite rate

## Why

- user can self-evaluate
- value is visible quickly
- buyer and user are often the same
- sales involvement would add friction too early
