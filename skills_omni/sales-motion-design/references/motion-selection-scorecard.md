# Motion Selection Scorecard

Score each segment separately.

## Candidate motions

- Pure PLG
- Hybrid product-led sales
- Sales-assisted PLG
- Sales-led

## Scoring dimensions

Rate each from 1 to 5.

| Dimension | 1 favors | 5 favors |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Self-serve feasibility | Sales-led | PLG |
| Time to first value | Slow / guided | Fast / independent |
| Buyer complexity | Multi-stakeholder | Single user/buyer |
| Security and procurement burden | Heavy | Light |
| Setup and integration burden | High | Low |
| Natural upgrade triggers | Weak | Strong |
| Marginal cost of free/trial users | High | Low |
| Need for human proof or persuasion | High | Low |
| Expansion potential from usage | Low | High |
| Sales value-add after usage | Critical | Optional |

## Interpretation

- Mostly 4-5: PLG or PLG-first hybrid is more likely
- Mixed 2-4: hybrid or sales-assisted is more likely
- Mostly 1-2: sales-led is more likely

## Required narrative output

For each segment, answer:

1. Recommended motion
2. Why this motion fits
3. What would break this motion
4. Confidence level
5. What data would change the recommendation

## Guardrail

Do not recommend one company-wide motion if the score meaningfully differs by segment.
