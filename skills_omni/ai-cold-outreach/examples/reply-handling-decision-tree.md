# Reply Handling Decision Tree

Use this to decide what happens after a recipient responds.

## Positive interest

Action:

- route quickly to a human owner or approved booking path
- preserve context from the original thread
- avoid restarting the pitch from scratch

## Question about offer

Action:

- answer directly
- keep the response short
- re-offer a low-friction next step

## Timing objection

Action:

- acknowledge timing
- ask permission for a later follow-up
- set a clear reminder if permitted

## Referral to another contact

Action:

- thank them
- ask for the best intro path or direct contact detail if appropriate
- update suppression rules for the original contact if needed

## Not interested / stop

Action:

- confirm removal
- suppress across all relevant systems
- do not continue sequence touches

## Out of office

Action:

- pause the sequence
- resume after the return date or a conservative waiting period

## Ambiguous or risky reply

Action:

- route to human review
- do not let automation guess in legal, angry, or reputation-sensitive threads
