# Operator Packets by Motion

Use these packets to tailor advice without rebuilding the entire workflow from scratch.

## Founder-led motion

Best when:

- low starting volume
- founder credibility matters
- narrow ICP
- fast learning loop is more important than scale

Priorities:

1. precise ICP
2. low daily volume
3. verified list quality
4. founder-native tone
5. fast reply handling

## SDR-led motion

Best when:

- repeatable segmentation exists
- reply handling can be staffed
- testing discipline is available

Priorities:

1. segmentation rules
2. sequence QA
3. suppression discipline
4. monitoring by segment and mailbox pool
5. objection-handling playbooks

## Agency-led motion

Best when:

- multiple clients or brands are involved
- white-label or shared operations create complexity

Priorities:

1. domain ownership clarity
2. client-by-client suppression boundaries
3. compliance routing by geography
4. stronger audit trails
5. explicit pause authority when deliverability worsens
