# Self-Reported Attribution Examples

## Form field options

### Option 1: simple

- How did you hear about us?

### Option 2: better

- How did you hear about us?
- What content, person, or channel influenced you most?

### Option 3: sales-assisted

Add to call opening:

- Before we start, what content of ours have you already seen, if any?
- Was there a post, video, newsletter, or referral that made this conversation more likely?

## Good answer categories

- LinkedIn post
- newsletter
- YouTube video
- podcast
- colleague referral
- community or Slack group
- search
- event or webinar
- other

## Notes

Keep free-text capture available when possible. Useful answers often appear in the details, not just the category.
