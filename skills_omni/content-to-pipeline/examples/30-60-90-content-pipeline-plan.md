# Example: 30/60/90 Content Pipeline Plan

## Scenario

- B2B service
- founder-led motion
- 4 hours per week available
- LinkedIn exists, newsletter does not

## Days 1-30

- choose LinkedIn as primary channel
- launch one weekly pillar post
- create 3 derivatives from each pillar
- add newsletter signup CTA to profile and posts
- define UTM naming and self-reported attribution field
- publish weekly and track baseline metrics

## Days 31-60

- launch simple newsletter
- add a 3-5 email welcome sequence
- test two CTA types: newsletter vs. framework download
- review comment quality, profile visits, signup rate, and replies
- remove low-performing topics

## Days 61-90

- review sourced leads and influenced opportunities
- compare themes by meeting creation
- refine CTA by awareness stage
- decide whether to keep one channel or add a second support channel

## Review rule

Do not add another major channel unless:

- cadence is sustainable
- capture path is working
- attribution is at least directionally reliable
