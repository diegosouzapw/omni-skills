# Content Pipeline Router

Use this note when the request drifts outside this skill's center of gravity.

## Stay in this skill when

- the task is content-led GTM design
- the task is channel focus, repurposing, CTA mapping, nurture, or attribution planning
- the task is diagnosing why content is not creating pipeline

## Route out when

- deep CRM implementation or analytics instrumentation is required
- paid acquisition or ad operations becomes the main work
- pricing or packaging becomes the main blocker
- outbound sequence design becomes the main conversion path
- legal privacy interpretation is required

## Suggested handoffs

- `@ai-cold-outreach` for follow-up sequence design
- `@ai-sdr` for outbound qualification systems
- `@ai-pricing` for packaging and value communication issues
- `@accessibility` for content artifact accessibility review
