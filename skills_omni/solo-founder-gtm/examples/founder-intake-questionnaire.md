# Founder Intake Questionnaire

Use this intake before recommending GTM changes.

## Product and Customer

1. What does the product do today in one paragraph?
2. What painful workflow does it solve?
3. Who is the ideal buyer or user?
4. What is the current strongest use case?

## Revenue and Motion

5. What is current MRR?
6. How many paying customers do you have?
7. What is average revenue per customer or ACV?
8. Is the current motion self-serve, founder-led sales, or mixed?

## Funnel and Channels

9. How are customers finding you today?
10. Which channel currently converts best?
11. What is your activation event?
12. How long does it take a new user to reach first value?

## Founder Capacity

13. How many hours per week do you spend on product, sales, support, content, and operations?
14. What manual task do you repeat most often?
15. What is your biggest bottleneck right now?

## Evidence and Constraints

16. What have you tried that did not work?
17. What objections or churn reasons come up most often?
18. What tools are currently in the stack and what do they cost?
19. Are there privacy, compliance, or customer-data constraints we should treat as hard limits?
20. What decision do you need to make in the next 14 days?
