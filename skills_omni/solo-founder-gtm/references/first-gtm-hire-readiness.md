# First GTM Hire Readiness

Hiring is a process-transfer problem before it is a budget problem.

## Minimum Readiness Criteria

- ICP is documented and specific.
- Main acquisition motion is already working at least somewhat repeatably.
- Qualification criteria are written down.
- CRM stages and definitions are stable enough to hand off.
- Common objections and responses are documented.
- The founder can explain onboarding and handoff steps clearly.
- There is sustained qualified demand that exceeds founder capacity.
- The bottleneck has persisted for multiple weeks, not just one busy week.

## Good Reasons to Delay Hiring

- Messaging still changes every week.
- The founder cannot define a qualified opportunity.
- Close rate is weak because discovery is poor.
- There is not enough demand to keep a contractor productive.
- The founder wants to hire to avoid learning the sales process.

## Contractor vs FTE

| Option | Better when |
| --- | --- |
| Contractor / fractional help | process exists but volume is uneven or still developing |
| Full-time hire | demand is steady, economics are clearer, and the handoff is well documented |

## Before Hiring

Prepare:

- example calls or transcripts
- objection log
- messaging guide
- pipeline stage definitions
- escalation rules
- success metrics for the first 30/60/90 days
