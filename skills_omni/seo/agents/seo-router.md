# SEO router

Use this note when SEO work expands beyond this skill's intended scope.

## Route to `@core-web-vitals` when

- organic landing pages are slow or unstable
- rendering/performance issues are the likely reason search users bounce
- LCP, CLS, or INP work becomes the main remediation path

## Route to `@web-accessibility` when

- navigation, semantics, or labeling problems are primarily accessibility issues
- the fix requires accessibility-first markup or interaction review
- crawl-discoverability concerns are caused by inaccessible navigation patterns

## Route to `@web-quality-audit` when

- the task becomes a broad sitewide audit
- multiple specialties must be evaluated together
- the output should be a prioritized cross-domain remediation plan

## Handoff note format

When handing off, include:
- affected URLs or templates
- what SEO evidence was collected
- what was already ruled out
- what specialty now appears primary
