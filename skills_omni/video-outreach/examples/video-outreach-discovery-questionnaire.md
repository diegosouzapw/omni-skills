# Video Outreach Discovery Questionnaire

Use this intake before recommending a motion, tool class, or sequence.

## Business context

1. What is the primary goal?
   - book meetings
   - accelerate active deals
   - replace low-value intro meetings
   - enable internal champion sharing

2. What is the approximate deal size or account value?

3. Which segment is being targeted?
   - SMB
   - mid-market
   - enterprise
   - mixed

## Volume and workflow

4. How many outreach videos are planned per week or month?

5. Is the team recording videos today or starting from scratch?

6. Is the motion primarily cold outbound, follow-up, recap, or demo-led?

## Stack and operations

7. Which CRM, sequencer, and video tools are already in use?

8. Is there a hosted landing page or video page available?

9. Can engagement events be logged into CRM today?

## Trust, language, and policy

10. Is the team comfortable using AI-generated likeness, avatar, or voice?

11. Are there policy restrictions on cloning, disclosure, or testimonial use?

12. Which languages are required?

13. Are any target regions or industries sensitive from a privacy or compliance perspective?

## Creative inputs

14. What can be shown in the first 15 seconds?
   - audit
   - teardown
   - screenshot
   - mini-demo
   - recap artifact
   - proposal angle

15. What is the single CTA?

16. What does success look like in 30 days?
   - play rate
   - reply rate
   - meetings booked
   - pipeline progression

## Operator note

If answers to policy, lawful basis, or disclosure questions are unclear, pause recommendation and escalate instead of forcing a tool choice.
