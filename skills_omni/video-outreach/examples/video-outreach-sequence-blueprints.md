# Video Outreach Sequence Blueprints

These are reusable starting patterns. Adapt them to the segment, channel policy, and deal size.

## 1. Cold outbound with useful artifact

### Day 1
- Email with thumbnail linking to a hosted 45-75 second video
- CTA: low-friction response or meeting if useful

### Day 3
- Text-only follow-up referencing the artifact

### Day 5
- LinkedIn touch or alternate channel follow-up if appropriate

### Day 8
- Short async demo or second asset if the product needs visual explanation

### Day 12
- Value-add email without video

### Day 15
- Light breakup note or short closing video

## 2. Async demo replacement

### Touch 1
- Short intro video explaining why the demo was recorded

### Touch 2
- Interactive demo link with one specific path to explore

### Touch 3
- Text follow-up offering to answer only the areas they care about

## 3. Post-demo recap

### Same day
- 2-4 minute recap video with the agreed next step

### 24 hours later
- Text summary with owners, timeline, and CTA

## Engagement branches

### No click
- test subject line or channel before changing the whole offer

### Click but low watch
- shorten the next video and move the value earlier

### High watch
- prioritize same-day follow-up within policy

### Shared internally
- send a recap asset or internal-forwarding version
