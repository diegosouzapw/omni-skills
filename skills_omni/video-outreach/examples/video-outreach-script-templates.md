# Video Outreach Script Templates

## 1. Human-recorded cold video

**Goal:** Book interest around a useful artifact.

```text
Hey [Name] — I was looking at [Company] and noticed [specific trigger or observation].

I put together a quick [audit / teardown / idea] for your team and wanted to show you the most relevant part.

[Show artifact immediately]

The short version is: [insight 1].
A second thing I noticed is: [insight 2].

If useful, I can walk you through the rest live or send the full version.
```

## 2. Hybrid AI-personalized opener plus human-led body

**Goal:** Add lightweight personalization while keeping the core message controlled.

```text
Hi [Name] — made this with [Company] in mind because [specific reason].

I want to show you one thing that stood out when I reviewed [site / workflow / use case].

[Show artifact]

Here is the practical takeaway: [one clear recommendation].

If it is relevant, I can send a deeper version or talk through it.
```

## 3. Async demo intro

**Goal:** Replace a low-value intro meeting.

```text
Hi [Name] — instead of making you sit through a generic intro call, I recorded a short walkthrough focused on [their use case].

I will show you [feature/path 1], [feature/path 2], and the part most relevant to [team or workflow].

If it looks useful, reply with the area you want to go deeper on and I will tailor the next step.
```

## 4. Post-demo recap

**Goal:** Reinforce alignment and next steps.

```text
Thanks again, [Name]. I recorded this short recap so you can share it internally.

The three things we aligned on were: [point 1], [point 2], and [point 3].

The recommended next step is [next step], and I have included the relevant resource below.
```

## Script guardrails

- one CTA only
- show the asset early
- remove filler openings
- keep claims factual and approved
- if synthetic generation materially changes the nature of the video, follow company disclosure policy
