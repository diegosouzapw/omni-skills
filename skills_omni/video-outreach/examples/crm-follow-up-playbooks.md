# CRM Follow-Up Playbooks

## 0% play

- Do not assume disinterest.
- Retry with a better subject line or another channel.
- Simplify the value proposition.

## 1-25% watch

- Send a short text recap.
- Reduce the next clip length.
- Move the key point earlier.

## 25-50% watch

- Follow up with one clarified takeaway.
- Consider a shorter role-specific clip.

## 50-75% watch

- Treat as meaningful engagement.
- Send one clear CTA.
- Prioritize in queue.

## 75%+ watch

- High-priority follow-up.
- Reference the exact topic covered.
- Offer a simple next step.

## Replay or share

- Assume internal interest may be forming.
- Send a recap, FAQ, ROI summary, or demo asset that can be forwarded.
- Ask if a tailored walkthrough for the wider team would help.
