# Voice Direction Templates

## Narration / explainer

```text
Voice Affect: Warm and composed.
Tone: Clear and helpful.
Pacing: Steady and moderate.
Emphasis: Stress the key action words.
```

## Product voiceover

```text
Voice Affect: Polished and confident.
Tone: Friendly and professional.
Pacing: Brisk but controlled.
Emphasis: Highlight product names and benefits.
```

## IVR / phone prompts

```text
Voice Affect: Neutral and reassuring.
Tone: Clear and direct.
Pacing: Slightly slow.
Pauses: Add brief pauses between menu choices.
Pronunciation: Enunciate numbers distinctly.
```

## Accessibility read

```text
Voice Affect: Calm and unobtrusive.
Tone: Clear and respectful.
Pacing: Steady, never rushed.
Pronunciation: Clarify acronyms, proper nouns, and abbreviations.
Pauses: Add short pauses at sentence boundaries.
```

## Iteration tip

When revising, keep one invariant line visible, for example:

```text
Pacing: Keep steady and moderate.
```
