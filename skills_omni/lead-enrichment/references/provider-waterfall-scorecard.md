## Scoring prompts

Score each provider from 1-5 for:

- fit for target segment
- incremental yield after earlier steps
- contact quality
- firmographic quality
- technographic quality
- auditability / provenance
- cost efficiency
- ease of exclusion and suppression handling

## Decision rules

- Lead with the provider that gives the best acceptable yield for the field at the lowest practical cost.
- Do not pay for expensive later steps unless earlier steps leave enough valuable records unresolved.
- Use different scorecards for contact fields and company fields if necessary.
- Re-score providers after QA and downstream performance data arrive.
