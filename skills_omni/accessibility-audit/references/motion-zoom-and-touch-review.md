# Motion, Zoom, and Touch Review

Use this worksheet for mobile, responsive, animation-heavy, or gesture-heavy interfaces.

## Motion

- Does the UI animate on load, hover, route change, open, close, or submit?
- Does reduced-motion preference reduce or remove non-essential animation?
- Does any animation create nausea, distraction, or tracking difficulty?

## Zoom and reflow

- At increased zoom, can the primary task still be completed?
- Does content reflow without hiding controls or creating two-direction task scrolling?
- Do dialogs, menus, and sticky elements remain reachable?

## Touch and target size

- Are controls large enough and spaced enough for touch use?
- Are dense action clusters prone to accidental taps?
- Are hover-only interactions still operable on touch devices?

## Gesture alternatives

- Does any feature require dragging, swiping, tracing, or a precise path?
- Is there a non-drag alternative unless the gesture is essential?

## Reporting reminders

- Flag issues that break task completion at mobile sizes as high severity.
- Record the tested viewport and orientation.
- Do not assume desktop keyboard coverage is enough for mobile accessibility risk.
