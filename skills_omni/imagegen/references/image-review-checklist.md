# Image Review Checklist

Use this checklist before handing off any generated or edited image.

## Core fidelity

- Does the image match the requested subject?
- Does the overall style match the requested medium or mood?
- Is the composition usable for the stated asset type?
- Are any required reference-image traits preserved?

## Text and branding

- If text was requested, is it exactly correct?
- Is text placement acceptable and legible?
- Are prohibited logos, marks, or watermarks absent?
- If brand-sensitive, are the required constraints still intact?

## Edit integrity

- Did the intended change happen and only that change?
- Were protected regions preserved?
- Are edges around edited regions clean?
- Is there any unintended drift in pose, product shape, or scene identity?

## Transparency and backgrounds

- If transparency was requested, does the file actually preserve alpha?
- Are there halos, matte edges, or cutout artifacts?
- Does the crop leave enough breathing room around the subject?
- Does the background replacement look consistent with lighting and perspective?

## Delivery quality

- Is the aspect ratio correct?
- Is the crop acceptable for the target placement?
- Is the selected final saved in the workspace if project-bound?
- Is the filename descriptive and non-destructive?
- Did you report the final prompt, tool path, and saved location?
