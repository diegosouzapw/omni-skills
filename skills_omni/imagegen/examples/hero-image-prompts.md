# Hero Image Prompt Examples

## Product hero

```text
Use case: product-mockup
Asset type: ecommerce landing page hero
Primary request: a premium stainless steel water bottle centered on a soft stone pedestal
Style/medium: clean product photography
Composition/framing: wide composition with generous negative space on the right for headline copy
Lighting/mood: bright studio lighting with soft shadows
Color palette: cool neutrals with subtle blue accents
Constraints: no logos, no text, no watermark
```
