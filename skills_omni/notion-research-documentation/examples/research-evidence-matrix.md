# Research Evidence Matrix Example

## Scenario

The user wants a report on why onboarding takes too long across multiple internal teams.

## Evidence matrix

| Claim | Source | Evidence | Confidence note | Contradiction/gap |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Each region maintains its own checklist | People Ops onboarding guide | "Regional onboarding steps are maintained locally by each team lead" | Medium; guide updated recently | Conflicts with central HR page claiming one global process |
| Engineering requires a separate access request workflow | Engineering new hire setup | "Managers must submit tool access requests after account creation" | High; operational doc | Adds delay not mentioned in HR docs |
| No single owner is named for cross-functional onboarding | HR process overview | No owner field or role is specified in the page | Medium; absence claim should be stated carefully | Could be defined outside Notion |

## Resulting synthesis

- Evidence shows onboarding is fragmented across regional and functional documentation.
- The contradiction between central HR and regional guidance should be flagged explicitly.
- The recommendation to assign a cross-functional owner is a judgment based on the evidence, not a direct source statement.
