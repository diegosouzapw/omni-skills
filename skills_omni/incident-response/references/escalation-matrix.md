# Escalation Matrix Template

| Severity | Trigger | Technical owner | Business owner | External comms |
| :------- | :------ | :-------------- | :------------- | :------------- |
| `SEV-1` | Broad customer impact or critical data path failure | Incident commander + service lead | Senior manager or on-call exec | Required |
| `SEV-2` | Major feature degradation with workaround | Service lead | Product or ops lead | Optional |
| `SEV-3` | Limited impact or contained subsystem issue | Primary responder | Team lead | Usually not needed |

Add phone, chat, and paging details next to each role in the active incident tool or runbook.
