# Pre-mortem Worksheet

Use this in **Find the Failure Modes** mode.

## Narrative starter

> It is six months later and this failed because...

## For each failure mode capture

- **Name:** Short memorable label
- **Trigger:** What started the failure?
- **Failure chain:** What happened step by step?
- **First-order impact:** Immediate technical or operational effect
- **Second-order impact:** Follow-on business, customer, or organizational effect
- **Detection signal:** What would reveal this early?
- **Blast radius:** How wide is the impact?
- **Readiness gap:** What capability or discipline was missing?

## Guidance

- Prefer 3-5 strong failure narratives.
- Do not list isolated risks without the consequence chain.
- Include detectability, not only severity.
- Avoid mitigation design here; keep the output critique-first.
