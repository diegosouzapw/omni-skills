# Confidentiality Review Checklist

Run this review before sharing a meeting packet broadly.

## Remove or minimize when not required

- Secrets, tokens, credentials, or internal-only operational details
- Personal data or sensitive personnel information
- Customer-identifying details beyond what attendees need
- Restricted roadmap details for audiences without a need to know
- Broad internal links that reveal unrelated sensitive context

## Verify

- The draft does not imply decisions that were not made.
- Sensitive content is limited to the minimum needed for the meeting.
- External research is cited and clearly separated from internal facts.
- Assumptions and open questions are labeled.
- The audience level matches the detail level of the packet.

## If needed

Produce two versions:
- a full internal packet
- a narrower shareable packet
