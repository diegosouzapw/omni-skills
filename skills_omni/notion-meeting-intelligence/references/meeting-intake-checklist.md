# Meeting Intake Checklist

Use this checklist before retrieval or drafting.

## Required intake

- Meeting objective
- Decisions needed or outcomes expected
- Attendees and their audience level
- Meeting type
- Duration and date/time
- Desired output artifact

## Context intake

- Canonical Notion pages or likely source locations
- Prior meeting notes
- Existing action items or decision logs
- Known blockers, risks, or dependencies
- Deadlines or approval windows

## Sensitivity intake

- Is the meeting confidential?
- Are HR, customer, security, legal, or roadmap topics involved?
- Should any attendee details be omitted from the shared draft?
- Is a narrower shareable version required?

## Readiness gates

Proceed only if one of the following is true:
- Notion access is working for the required pages
- the user provided a fallback packet with enough source material

Do not proceed with invented internal facts when both are false.
