# Meeting Source Matrix Template

Use this template to track what was retrieved and how it should influence the meeting packet.

| Source | Link | Owner | Freshness | What it supports | Confidence | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Example: Q2 churn review | notion://... or page URL | Revenue Ops | Updated 2026-03-20 | Pricing context, churn causes | High | Canonical review deck notes |
| Example: Packaging experiment summary | notion://... or page URL | PM | Updated 2026-03-18 | Option tradeoffs | Medium | Awaiting final finance sign-off |

## Guidance

- Prefer canonical or owner-maintained pages.
- Mark stale or indirect sources as medium or low confidence.
- Do not rely on search snippets alone when a full page can be fetched.
- Keep this matrix with the draft so reviewers can trace major claims.
