# Handoff Router

Use this note when the work is no longer mainly about meeting preparation.

## Hand off to a research-oriented skill when
- external analysis becomes the majority of the work
- the user wants a benchmark report more than a meeting packet
- source evaluation and citation depth are the primary deliverable

## Hand off to a documentation-oriented skill when
- the output must become a durable memo, PRD, decision record, or formal documentation set
- editing quality, structure, and long-term maintainability matter more than meeting readiness

## Hand off to a planning or product skill when
- the meeting packet has turned into roadmap, scope, milestone, or execution planning
- the main task is prioritization or delivery planning rather than preparation for discussion

## Hand off to a security review skill when
- the content involves incidents, vulnerabilities, access controls, compliance, or restricted systems
- confidentiality and risk analysis dominate the work

## Minimum packet for handoff

Provide:
- meeting objective
- audience and attendees
- current draft
- source matrix
- unresolved questions
- confidentiality constraints
- whether Notion write access is available
