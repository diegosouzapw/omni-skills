# Example: Executive Decision Pre-read with Citations

## Request

Prepare a pricing review pre-read using Notion pages for churn analysis, packaging experiments, and sales feedback. Add external SaaS pricing benchmarks only if they materially improve the discussion.

## Output shape

### Objective
Decide whether to approve the proposed packaging change for the next quarter.

### Internal context
- Churn rose in two target segments after the last pricing update. [Notion page link]
- Sales feedback shows repeated confusion around seat limits. [Notion page link]
- The latest experiment improved expansion in one segment but hurt conversion in another. [Notion page link]

### External research
- Peer pricing pages suggest clearer packaging boundaries reduce evaluation friction in similar SaaS motions. [External source link]
- Benchmark commentary indicates approval risk rises when migration terms are unclear. [External source link]

### Recommendation
Approve a narrower packaging change only if migration terms and customer communications are finalized before launch.

### Open questions
- Which customer segment should be excluded from the first rollout?
- Is finance aligned on downside tolerance?

### Agenda
1. Context review - 10 min
2. Options and tradeoffs - 15 min
3. Decision and conditions - 15 min
4. Owners and next steps - 5 min
