# Example: One-on-One Agenda from Notion Context

## Request

Build a manager-report one-on-one agenda from prior Notion notes and open follow-ups.

## Suggested output

### Objective
Use the meeting to review follow-ups, unblock work, and discuss growth support.

### Agenda
1. Follow-ups from last meeting
2. Current blockers and support needed
3. Progress on current priorities
4. Feedback in both directions
5. Career growth or development topic
6. Confirm next actions

### Notes
- Keep private or sensitive details out of the agenda unless necessary.
- Prefer concise prompts over long summaries.
- Link to prior notes rather than copying large private excerpts.
