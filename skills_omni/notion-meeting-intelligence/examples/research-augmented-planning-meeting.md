# Example: Research-Augmented Planning Meeting

## Request

Prepare a planning brief for a support automation project using Notion project materials and external research on rollout risks.

## Suggested output

### Internal context
- Project charter defines scope, goals, and owners. [Notion link]
- Backlog review identifies the top workflow gaps. [Notion link]
- Recent incident notes show failure patterns that must be addressed before rollout. [Notion link]

### External research
- Industry writeups indicate exception routing and human fallback are common adoption safeguards. [External source link]
- Benchmark material suggests rollout friction is highest when edge cases are excluded from pilot plans. [External source link]

### Open questions
- Which exceptions require human review from day one?
- What rollout metric determines pilot success?

### Meeting agenda
1. Confirm scope and success criteria
2. Review risk areas from incidents
3. Evaluate rollout safeguards
4. Resolve ownership and timeline
