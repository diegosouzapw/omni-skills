## Review timeline

### 180 days before renewal

- Risk review complete?
- Success criteria documented?
- Stakeholder map current?
- Expansion opportunities identified?

### 120 days before renewal

- QBR delivered?
- ROI or value summary prepared?
- Procurement process understood?
- Commercial changes anticipated?

### 90 days before renewal

- Renewal conversation started?
- Risks explicitly documented?
- Economic buyer engaged?
- Proposed terms prepared?

### 60 days before renewal

- Legal / procurement blockers identified?
- Competing vendor risk assessed?
- Save plan or expansion plan updated?

### 30 days before renewal

- Signature path clear?
- Escalation owner assigned?
- Any unresolved product or billing objections?
