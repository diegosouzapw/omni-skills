# Closed-Lost Re-engagement Sequences

Use these as pattern examples, not mandatory scripts.

## Lost reason: timing / not now

### Day 30
- Brief check-in
- Share one relevant product or market update
- No hard pitch

### Day 90
- Revisit whether timing changed
- Offer a short reassessment call

## Lost reason: product gap

### After gap is materially closed
- Explain what changed
- Connect the change to the original blocked use case
- Offer a focused re-demo

## Lost reason: chose competitor

### Triggered re-entry
- Re-engage only if there is a concrete reason such as a major product improvement, a new stakeholder, or a changed business requirement
- Avoid generic “just checking in” outreach

## Lost reason: budget

### Planning-cycle outreach
- Align contact to the customer budgeting window
- Lead with ROI clarity, not urgency

## Guardrails

- do not over-contact
- document the lost reason and reason for re-entry
- route personalized outreach to sales/account owner
- stop if the account asks not to be contacted
